ABOUT US:

K. Ramakrishnan College of Engineering (KRCE), Tiruchirappalli was established in 2008, affiliated to AU,
Chennai and approved by AICTE. It offers various undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the stream
of Engineering, Technology and Management. Admission to KRCE Trichy is done on the basis of TANCET
and TNEA scores. In addition to this, the institute has a dedicated training and placement cell. Some of the
top recruiters are Infosys, Justdial, L&T Infotech, TCS, Amazon, Wipro, ICICI Bank, etc.
